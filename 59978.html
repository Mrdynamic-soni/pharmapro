Brief Summary  

This is a randomized, open-label, two-arm study of the combination of homoeopathic medications as add-on therapy in patients with severe to critical COVID-19 infection being conducted at AIIMS Bhopal. The objective of this study is to assess the efficacy and safety of a homoeopathic medication combination as add-on therapy in severe COVID 19 patients, as assessed by the Clinical Severity-Ordinal scale, the National Early Warning Score (NEWS), oxygenation, mechanical ventilation, hospitalisation, and mortality. Additionally, to assess changes in inflammatory markers.
